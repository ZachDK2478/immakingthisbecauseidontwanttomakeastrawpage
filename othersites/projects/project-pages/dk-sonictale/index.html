DK!SONICTALE

DK!SONICTALE is an UNDERTALE AU (Alternate Universe) that takes Sonic the Hedgehog characters and places them into UNDERTALE's world. That's the simple part. Now to get more in-depth.

After a brutal battle against the Eggman Empire, Sonic and his friends were on the backfoot. Cream was attacked, but fortunately Knuckles managed to use the Master Emerald to shield her.

As Sonic lunged towards Dr. Eggman's cockpit, a strange, warbling sound was heard.

In one moment, everything they knew and loved was gone.
Their homes.
Their lives.
Everything.
They all ended up in a world unknown to them, but all too familiar to us. The Underground.

Sonic retired from heroism, even his unshakable optimism dimming due to the circumstances. His home with Tails lies deep in the forest of Snowdin.

Tails focused all of his efforts on finding a way home, almost to an obsessive degree, just wanting to make things right. With him, he holds a Chaos Emerald.

Knuckles guards a Chaos Emerald, far into the depths of Hotland. Knuckles calls Hotland "Lava Reef" because he misses his home.

Amy helps Vanilla touch up the Ruins, now known as the Chao Sanctuary, into a paradise. Vanilla holds a Chaos Emerald.

Shadow took charge in place of Sonic, guarding a Chaos Emerald within New Home, now called the Last Bastion.

Rouge, following Shadow's orders, was stationed in a Waterfall settlement. She guards a Chaos Emerald of her own.

Dr. Eggman lies in a lab around where Lava Reef/Hotland ends, guarding the sixth Chaos Emerald and constantly experimenting with ways to return home.

The cooling molten rock allows for easier analysis of the enviornment, while he can just take energy from the geothermal energy.

Jet lies above most areas in a location simply called the Horizon, holding the final Chaos Emerald.
